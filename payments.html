Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following secure payment options:

  • Bank Transfer (within Cyprus or international)
  • Cash (in-person only)

Advance Payment Policy

All services must be paid for in advance. We do not begin any operation, patrol, or escort without cleared funds unless explicitly agreed in writing for repeat clients.

Retainer Plans

Monthly retainers start from €200/month and include priority access and standby availability. Specific coverage and terms are outlined during onboarding. Retainers must be prepaid at the beginning of each billing cycle.

Cancellation & Refunds

Cancellations made less than 24 hours before service may be subject to full or partial charge. No refunds are available after service has commenced.

Invoices & Receipts

All clients will receive a digital invoice or receipt. Business clients may request VAT invoices where applicable.
